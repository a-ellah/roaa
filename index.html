04. Works

The Ballara play

Three cousins—Salim, Mona, and Hossam—gather at their late grandfather's house to open his inheritance, only to find an old red ball. Initially dismissing it, they later discover a mysterious inscription on the ball, leading them to uncover a family secret hidden in the house. They find a secret compartment containing important documents and jewels. In the end, they realize their grandfather wanted to test their unity, not intelligence, showing them that the true inheritance is their family bond, not material wealth. The red ball symbolizes this lesson.

A Documentary on the Life of Monkeys

This documentary explores the fascinating lives of monkeys, showcasing their intelligence, social structures, and survival skills in diverse habitats. From the dense jungles to the urban landscapes, the film captures their daily routines, communication methods, and interactions within their groups. Through stunning cinematography and expert insights, the documentary provides a deeper understanding of these remarkable primates and their crucial role in the ecosystem.

Jijel une destination touristique incroyable

Jijel Province is one of the most prominent tourist destinations in Algeria, combining enchanting nature with diverse activities. It boasts charming beaches, such as the Red Beach and the Beach of the Marvelous Caves, along with dense forests, such as the Ziama Mansouria Forest, which offer opportunities for adventure and camping. The Tamerza Zoo also boasts natural attractions, such as the Ourit Waterfalls, which offer unique moments of relaxation. Whether you're looking for tranquility or exploration, Jijel offers a unique tourist experience that combines natural beauty with a variety of activities.
